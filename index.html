I’m an Environmental Geologist focused on understanding how land cover and human activity influence soil and water quality. My work integrates field and laboratory data with GIS and remote sensing to analyze spatial patterns, interpret geochemical processes, and communicate results through clear, publication-ready visuals.

Tools I regularly use include ArcGIS Pro, QGIS, Excel, and satellite-based remote sensing workflows (e.g., Sentinel-2).
